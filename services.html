About Naj Daniel

Naj Daniel is a seasoned social media manager and strategist with over eight years of experience building and elevating digital brands across East Africa. Known for blending data-driven strategies with bold creative execution, Naj has successfully led campaigns that drive engagement, brand loyalty, and measurable business growth.

Throughout his career, Naj has partnered with leading institutions such as Equity Bank, PostBank, Netflix Kenya, Pulse Uganda, Crown Beverages Uganda, and business magnate Sudhir Ruparelia.
